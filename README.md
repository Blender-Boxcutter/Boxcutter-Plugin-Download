## 🧠 Overview

**Boxcutter** is a specialized Blender addon created to streamline hard surface modeling through fast and interactive boolean operations. It enables users to build complex shapes by directly drawing cutters and applying modifications in real time, significantly reducing the need for traditional modeling steps.

The plugin focuses on efficiency, allowing artists to quickly block out forms and refine details without interrupting their workflow. Instead of manually setting up modifiers and adjusting geometry, Boxcutter provides an immediate, visual approach to shaping models.

Its toolset is designed for precision and flexibility. Users can easily switch between different cutting modes, control depth and alignment, and experiment with various shapes while maintaining full control over the final result. This makes it ideal for creating detailed mechanical objects, architectural elements, and stylized assets.

Boxcutter integrates naturally into Blender’s workspace, complementing existing tools and workflows. Its responsive controls and non-destructive behavior allow for continuous adjustments, making it easier to iterate and improve designs without starting from scratch.

Whether used for rapid prototyping or detailed asset creation, **Boxcutter** offers a modern and efficient approach to hard surface modeling.
